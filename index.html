Introduction

Welcome to Clutch State. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our Facebook Page (https://web.facebook.com/clutchstate26). Our page shares NBA updates, memes, and parody content. By interacting with our page (liking, commenting, messaging, or using features), you agree to the practices described here.

What data we collect

  • Data on Facebook: We do not operate Facebook’s systems. Any data you provide through Facebook (likes, comments, messages, reactions, shares) is stored and controlled by Facebook. We do not separately collect that data outside Facebook unless you send it to us directly (e.g., via Messenger or form).
  • Public interactions: Public comments, reactions, and shares on posts are visible to other Facebook users and may be viewable to us on the page admin tools.
  • Messages: If you send us a direct message, the content of the message is visible to our page admins. We may retain messages to respond and improve our service.
  • Analytics: We may view aggregated, anonymized analytics provided by Facebook (insights) such as page reach, impressions, demographic summaries, and engagement metrics. These are used to improve content and posting times.

How we use collected data

  • To respond to messages and comments from followers.
  • To post, promote, and improve content — including NBA updates, memes, and parody posts.
  • To analyze engagement and tailor content via Facebook Page Insights.
  • To moderate comments and enforce community standards.

Cookies and similar technologies

We do not place cookies on your device directly through this Facebook Page. However, Facebook and third-party services used by Facebook may set and use cookies and similar technologies. Please review Facebook’s Cookie Policy and settings for details on how cookies are used and how you can manage them through your Facebook account.

Third-party services and links

Our page may include links to third-party websites or embedded content (videos, external articles, or tools). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. If you follow external links, we recommend you review the privacy policies of those sites.

Advertising & promotions

If we run promotions, giveaways, or sponsored posts, any data collected for those activities (e.g., entries, winner contact) will be used only for administering the promotion unless otherwise stated. Promotions may be subject to Facebook’s promotion guidelines.

How long we keep data

We retain direct messages and any information you provide until it's no longer needed for the purpose collected (for example, to answer your inquiry), unless a longer retention period is required by law. Aggregated analytics from Facebook are retained according to Facebook’s policies.

Children

Our content is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

Your rights

  • Access & control: You can review or delete comments and other content you posted on our page via your Facebook account.
  • Contact & request: For requests related to data visible to us (for example, messages you sent to the page), please contact us through our Facebook Page.
  • Opt-out: You may control notifications from our page through your Facebook settings and can unfollow or unlike the page at any time.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect information accessible to page admins. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information seen or sent via Facebook.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Material changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. Continued use of our page after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

How to contact us

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us via our Facebook Page messaging: https://web.facebook.com/clutchstate26
